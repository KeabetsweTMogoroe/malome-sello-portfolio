About Malome Sello

Malome Sello is part of Mmasemanodi, a project dedicated to developing and supporting premium livestock farmers across South Africa. Through innovative training and hands-on guidance, the project helps farmers deliver quality cattle, sheep, and goats while building sustainable practices.

Malome is committed to excellence, ensuring every animal is nurtured and managed professionally, contributing to the growth of Mmasemanodi and the empowerment of local farmers.
